About Us


WHAT WE AIM TO


"15 YEARS OF MOVIES"is an independent film magazine and website run by volunteers.
They cover the best in independent and festival cinema year round and every October are officially responsible for the full,
comprehensive coverage of the OSCARS.
We take pride in providing comprehensive and good film reviews.




OUR VALUES


We strive to improve everything we do by adopting new technologies and ways of working, improving what we offer our customers, and looking for new technologies and services
